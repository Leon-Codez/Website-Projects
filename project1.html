Taco Recipe

Assemble the Ingredients and Combine for a burst of flavor !

Ingredients

  • Taco Shell
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Hot Sauce
  • Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Cook Ground Beef to your liking. Tutorial on Taco Ground Beef Linked HERE
  2. Put ground beef in taco shell
  3. Put cheese in taco shell ontop of ground beef
  4. Put lettuce in taco ontop of cheese
  5. Put sour cream ontop of lettuce
  6. Finish off with hot sauce of your choice
